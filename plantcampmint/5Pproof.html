5 Dollar Proof Coin

The proof 5 dollar coin starts out the same way as normal circulating coins, but after they are baked, an inspector hand-picks the best coins. Those coins are then hand coated with nail polish, to give it a soft, shiny appearance. These coins are then packed up in a high-quality plant holder and sold. These pieces are legal tender for face value.
